# YouTube to Shorts Editing Kit

YouTube to Shorts editing turns long-form videos into short, vertical, platform-ready clips. This repository collects templates and checklists for selecting moments, rewriting hooks, adding captions, planning voiceover, and adapting long YouTube videos into Shorts, Reels, TikTok clips, and social posts.

The goal is to make long-video repurposing easier to plan before editing begins.

## Repurposing formats

| Format | Best for | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Key insight clip | Tutorials, interviews, webinars | One useful idea in 30-60 seconds |
| Story highlight | Vlogs, documentaries, recaps | A compact story moment |
| Problem-solution clip | Product education, how-to videos | Pain point and answer |
| Reaction clip | Commentary and entertainment | Strong opinion or moment |
| Series teaser | Long episodes and channels | Open loop that leads back to source |

## Editing workflow

1. Map the source video: title, duration, chapters, topic, and audience.
2. Identify clip candidates: moments with strong hook, standalone context, and platform fit.
3. Rewrite the opening: start with outcome, conflict, question, or visual surprise.
4. Trim context: keep only what the viewer needs to understand the moment.
5. Add captions and visual emphasis.
6. Create variants for Shorts, Reels, TikTok, and paid social.
7. Review for pacing, clarity, and next action.
