# Shorts Repurposing Checklist

## Before editing

- Source video has been watched or summarized.
- Chapters or topic segments are identified.
- Candidate moments are scored.
- Target platform and audience are defined.
- Desired output length is clear.

## During editing

- The first 3 seconds create curiosity.
- The clip can be understood without the full video.
- Captions are readable on mobile.
- The crop preserves faces, objects, and visual focus.
- The ending gives a reason to continue, click, or watch more.

## After editing

- Title or caption matches the clip promise.
- The clip does not overpromise.
- The source video link or next action is clear when needed.
- Platform-specific safe areas are respected.
